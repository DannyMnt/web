Here is a short description of each type of project

Residential projects

Residential projects consists of small to large sized houses with a maximum budget of $500.000. We can also renovate a house, not just build it from 0!

Comercial projects

Comercial projects mainly consists of offices built for companies and enterprieses with a maximum budget of $2.000.000 but the intended use of the building must be provided.

Industrial projects

Industrial projects consists of factories, warehouses, powerplants and other types of buildings with a maximum budget of $10.000.000.

Roads projects

Roads are one of the most important elements of an infrastracture due to the rising numbers of vehicles sold and so, we are providing the best roads ever built, from simple one lane roads to masive highways, tunnels and bridges!
